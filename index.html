Kaiju No. 8

The story follows a man named Kafka Hibino who gains the power of a kaiju, fights to prove his humanity to the Japan Defense Force and fulfill a promise he made with his childhood friend.

Demon Slayer

Set in Taisho era Japan, the story follows Tanjiro Kamado, who finds his entire family massacred, finding his younger sister turned to a demon, he asks for the Demon Slayer Corps help to turn back his sister into a human.

Jujutsu Kaisen

This manga is set in a world where entities known as cursed spirits prey on the people of Japan. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a student that has superhuman strength accidentally swallows a cursed object, which is the finger of Ryomen Sukuna, the strongest demon in all of history.

Solo Leveling

This manhwa follows the life of the weakest hunter of mankind, Sung Jinwoo. In a world where portals to different dimension filled with monsters have appeared and threaten mankind's existence. After surviving a double dungeon, he becomes the only hunter that gets stronger after each battle.

The Beginning After The End

This is a story about King Grey, someone who has undoubtedly reached the peak of his kingdom and unrivaled in strength and wealth. As he is reincarnated into a new world full of magic and monsters, living his new life with purpose and full of emotions is his goal.
